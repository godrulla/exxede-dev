Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience. "Local storage" is a similar browser technology that stores data locally on your device.

2. How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

Exxede.dev uses a minimal, privacy-first approach to cookies and browser storage. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

2.1 What We Store

Storage Key Type Purpose Data Stored Duration
exxede-theme localStorage Remember your dark/light mode preference "dark" or "light" Until cleared
exxede-lang localStorage Remember your language preference "en" or "es" Until cleared

Both items are strictly functional — they contain no personal data, no identifiers, and no tracking information.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to each service's privacy policy for details.

4. No Tracking or Advertising

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts
  • We do not serve targeted advertisements
  • We do not create user profiles or behavioral fingerprints
  • We do not share browsing data with advertisers
  • We do not use cross-site tracking

5. Managing Your Preferences

5.1 Our Storage

You can clear our local storage data at any time:

  • Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
  • Go to Application > Local Storage
  • Delete the exxede-theme and exxede-lang entries

Or simply clear your browser data for this site. Your theme and language will reset to defaults (dark mode, English).

5.2 Browser-Level Controls

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies and local storage
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences on a per-site basis
  • Browse in private/incognito mode (no persistent storage)

Note: Blocking local storage will reset your theme and language preferences on each visit but will not affect core functionality.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we add new cookies or storage mechanisms, we will update this page. Significant changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date.
