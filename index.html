Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to build a web application?

A focused MVP takes 4-6 weeks. A full-featured platform with admin dashboards, payments, and AI integration typically runs 8-16 weeks. We structure projects in clear phases so you see working software every week, not at the end. Most clients see a working demo within 10 business days.

What's your pricing model?

We price by project scope with clear phases and deliverables — not by the hour. Each project begins with a free discovery call and a written estimate that specifies exactly what you get. We offer both fixed-price projects and monthly retainers for ongoing development.

Do you work with startups or established companies?

Both. We love working with startups — we've helped several go from napkin sketch to funded product. For startups, we focus on lean MVPs that validate fast. For established companies, we build enterprise-grade platforms. Either way, you get direct access to senior-level builders on every project.

Can you integrate AI into our existing product?

Yes — AI integration is one of our core specialties. We integrate OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), Groq, and local models (Ollama) depending on your cost, latency, and privacy requirements. Common AI features we build: intelligent search, chatbots, content generation, document analysis, and multi-agent workflows.

What payment gateways can you integrate?

We have direct experience integrating Stripe (global), Azul / Banco Popular (DR local), PayPal, and others. For Dominican Republic businesses, local gateway support is a critical capability most offshore agencies lack.

Do you handle both design and development?

Yes. We own the full product lifecycle: UI/UX design, development, testing, deployment, and monitoring. You work with one team throughout. No account managers routing your requests through junior developers — faster communication, faster decisions, faster delivery.

What happens after launch?

We offer ongoing retainer engagements — bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature additions, and infrastructure management. We don't disappear after deployment. Many of our clients keep us on for continuous improvement.

Do you build mobile apps?

We specialize in Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) — web applications that install on mobile devices, work offline, and feel native. For most use cases, a well-built PWA outperforms a rushed React Native app at a fraction of the cost.
