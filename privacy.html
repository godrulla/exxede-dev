1. Introduction

Exxede.dev ("we," "our," or "us") is a web application development studio operated by Armando Diaz Silverio, based in Punta Cana, Dominican Republic. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website exxede.dev or engage our services.

By using our website or submitting information through our contact form, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you fill out our contact form, we collect:

  • Name — to address you personally
  • Email address — to respond to your inquiry
  • Project type — to understand your needs
  • Budget range — to provide an appropriate estimate
  • Message content — your project description and requirements

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, scroll depth
  • IP address — for analytics and security purposes
  • Referral source — how you found our website

2.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode) — key: exxede-theme
  • Language preference (EN/ES) — key: exxede-lang

These are strictly functional and contain no personal data. See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide project estimates
  • Communicate about potential or ongoing projects
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure website security
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, profiling, or targeted advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Their privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy

  • Google Fonts — serves web fonts. Google may collect usage data.
  • Cloudflare CDN — delivers JavaScript libraries (Three.js, Vanta.js)

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for up to 2 years for business records. You may request deletion at any time by emailing godrulla@gmail.com.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at godrulla@gmail.com.

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.
